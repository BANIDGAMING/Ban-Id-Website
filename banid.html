📌 ImGui Mod Menu Usage Guide

Follow these steps carefully to avoid bans and trigger anti-cheat systems.

🔧 Setup Requirements:

  • DNS Bypass + Server Bypass must be active before launching the game.
  • Offline Protection:
    • Disable Free Fire via App Manager when not in use.
    • Clear cache after every session.
    • Disable ImGui ModMenu from the module section when not playing.

🎮 In-Game Usage Rules:

🚫 When to Enable Hacks:

✅ CS (Clash Squad) Match:

  • Enable hacks ONLY during the Shope Timer
  • Do NOT enable mid-match.

✅ BR (Battle Royale) Match:

  • Enable hacks ONLY inside the plane (before jumping).
  • Do NOT enable after landing.

❌ Strict Prohibitions:

  • Never enable hacks during an ongoing match.
  • After finishing a match, TURN OFF all hacks before restarting.
  • DO NOT return to the lobby—restart the match directly.
  • If playing in a squad, TELL YOUR TEAMMATES NOT TO RETURN TO LOBBY (or risk blacklist).

🛡️ Anti-Cheat Avoidance Rules:

To prevent detection, follow these limits:

  • 🔹 BR Match: Do not exceed 15 kills.
  • 🔹 CS Match: Do not exceed 11 kills.
  • 🔹 No Sky Kills (Do not kill enemies while in the air).
  • 🔹 No Rapid Kills: Max 4 kills per scope time (Auto-aim locks onto next enemy after a down).

🔄 Re-Entry Protocol:

  • First Launch: Open Free Fire WITHOUT hacks for one match.
  • Second Launch: Activate Server Bypass + DNS Bypass before enabling the mod menu.
  • Never open Free Fire with the mod menu active without bypass protection.
